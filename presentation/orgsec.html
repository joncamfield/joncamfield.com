* Build strategic next steps * SAFETAG style * enterprise/hybrid/firefighter models OrgSec is not individuals independently managing their own risk, nor is it only reactive, after an event.

But - Why ?

SAFETAG is the cornerstone of our approach to digital security; "Organizational change framework hiding inside a penetration test" "firercely pragmatic security" Focus on realistic assessment of threats, identification of vulnerabilities -- but more importantly, an understanding of mission, priorities and redlines, and organizational capacity. Identification of high-impact / low-lift tasks to move on quickly, and roadmap out projects along a realistic timeline based on their impact and complexity (in terms of testing/training/funding/compliance needs) This process applies at the org level, but we also recommend adapting it to program design and community interactions.
